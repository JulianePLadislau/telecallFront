Mask Number

Masked Number

Protect professional identities

Ensure your customers have the ability to make calls and send messages without exposing their personal phone numbers.

  • Mask a phone number for interactions with more privacy.
  • Allows businesses to conduct operations using fewer phone numbers.
  • Provides a more secure and professional experience.

How does it work?


  • User makes a call through an application. (Example: user calls a delivery driver or taxi driver or contacts a customer service center.)
  • Platform masks the original number. (The platform receives the call and masks the number before connecting with the recipient.)
  • Both parties are connected. (The platform connects both parties while maintaining the privacy of both.)


Who uses it?

Transportation Apps

Allows drivers and passengers to communicate without sharing their personal numbers.

Dating Apps

Provides a private and secure way for users to interact without exposing personal contacts.

E-Commerce

Allows customers to contact you through the application. It can also integrate international calls.

Deliveries & Logistics

Keep your customers informed about deliveries and services, answer questions, etc.

Who else can use it?

43% of Brazilian companies have adopted Remote Work as the standard.

Even after the end of the pandemic, statistics indicate that the Remote Work model is expected to grow by about 30%.

Employees who do not have a company phone or virtual extension can benefit from using a permanent mask number linked to their personal cell phone, thus protecting their personal numbers.

Advanced Features

  • SMS Mask number is fully functional for voice calls and SMS.
  • Geo Match Match the country code of the mask number with that of the originating call, giving the impression to the customer that you are in the same region.
  • Session Management Enable permanent mask numbers, block unwanted calls, session validity, and more.
  • Reports Direct customer access to detailed reports.
  • Concurrent Numbers Use the same mask number in multiple concurrent calls.
  • Content Screening SMS feature where you can flag sensitive content to protect customer data.
  • Unlimited Scalability Purchase and add numbers as needed.
