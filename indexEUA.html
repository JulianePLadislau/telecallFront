    CPaaS: What is it?

    Communications Platform as a Service

    It is a communication software solution that serves as a foundation upon which developers can integrate a variety of applications.

    Typical communication methods such as voice, video calls, or SMS text messages can be incorporated into other systems through APIs that connect to the CPaaS platform.

    These APIs allow companies to expand their offerings without the need for additional hardware or software.
